# Research Report Writing Skill

Use this skill when turning paper analysis into a polished research reading report.

## Workflow

1. Start with basic paper information and a concise summary of the research problem.
2. Explain the background and research gap before describing the method.
3. Present the method in a structured way: core idea, pipeline, and assumptions.
4. Summarize experiments with datasets, baselines, metrics, and main findings.
5. Discuss limitations and validity concerns separately from the authors' conclusions.
6. Propose follow-up research ideas grounded in the paper's evidence and limitations.
7. Keep claims traceable to extracted paper content; mark inference clearly.

## Output Shape

Prefer Markdown reports with:

- Basic Info
- Research Background
- Research Gap
- Method Analysis
- Experiment Analysis
- Results and Conclusion
- Limitations
- Research Discussion Questions
- Research Follow-up Ideas
- Concise Research Summary
