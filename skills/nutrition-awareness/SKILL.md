# Nutrition Awareness Skill

Use this skill when a recipe task includes dietary preferences, avoid lists, or general health-awareness notes.

## Workflow

1. Respect avoid lists and dietary preferences provided by the user.
2. Keep nutrition notes general and non-medical.
3. Do not claim to diagnose, treat, or manage a medical condition.
4. Flag common concerns such as high salt, added sugar, or oil only as general awareness.
5. Suggest simple substitutions when they improve fit with user preferences.
6. Encourage users with medical needs to follow professional advice.

## Output Shape

Prefer concise notes:

- dietaryFit
- avoidListHandled
- generalNutritionNotes
- substitutionNotes
- safetyDisclaimer
