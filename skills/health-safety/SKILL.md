# Health Safety Skill

Health-related outputs must stay within an informational scope.

Always include:

1. The output is for health information organization and learning only.
2. It cannot replace a licensed clinician's diagnosis, treatment, or prescription.
3. Users should seek medical care for acute symptoms, severe abnormalities, or uncertainty.

Avoid:

- Diagnosing a disease from a single report
- Recommending medication, dosage, or treatment changes
- Claiming a result is safe without clinical context
- Storing or exposing private health data in Git-tracked files
